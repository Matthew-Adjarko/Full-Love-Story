Setting

The story begins entirely online.

One evening, Ethan is scrolling through Snapchat when he comes across a story posted by a mutual friend. The story features Amara laughing with her friends during a birthday celebration.

Something about her smile catches his attention.

Without thinking too much about it, Ethan taps "Add Friend."

A few minutes later...

Accepted.

He hesitates before replying to one of her stories.

His message is simple.

"Do I know you?"

Neither of them realizes that this ordinary message will change both of their lives forever.

The story gradually moves from Snapchat chats to voice notes, late-night calls, video calls, and eventually their first meeting in person. What starts as casual conversations slowly grows into friendship, trust, and a love they never expected.

Was this just another friend request... or the beginning of a love story written by destiny?
